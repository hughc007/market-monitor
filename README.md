# Australian Energy & Commodity Market Monitor

This project is an Australian energy and commodity market monitor focused on key market drivers and regional equity exposure. It tracks:

- Brent Crude (BZ=F)
- WTI Crude (CL=F)
- AUD/USD (AUDUSD=X)
- Woodside Energy (WDS.AX)
- Santos (STO.AX)

The monitor performs spread, volatility, and correlation analysis, generates rule-based trading signals, and produces automated desk-style market commentary.

## Project Scope

- commodity, FX, and equity instrument configuration
- historical data ingestion and analysis
- signal generation for spread, volatility, correlation, and FX moves
- output generation for charts, notes, and market commentary
